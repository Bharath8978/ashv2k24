Description


Precepts

1. Solo/Troop participants.
2. Troop may have a minimum of 2.
3. Either own composition or inherited one.
4. Time slot for each performance is 4 min only.
5. Decision of judges is final.


Classical

It's an art of arranging sounds in time so as to produce a continuous, unified and evocative composition, more than that of melody, harmony, rhythm through vocal voice. Bring your Shruti, Swara, Raga & Tala together and get us view the music lord virtually.So, put up a fight against vocal voices. Express your Feelings through voice ferociously ......


Folk

The music which represents a community and can be sung by people either by solo / group who may or may not actually be trained musicians, by using instruments available to them.Make a stand against the people and participate...


Contemporary - Solo / Group

Describes the style of music that represents the current issues (already existing).It's predominantly heavy music of the present day with varying degrees of easy listening, soul and rhythm. Show your mighty skills and make us to sing along with you.


A Cappella (without BGM)

It is specifically a group or solo singing without instrumental accompaniment, or a piece intended to be performed in this way. It contrasts with cantata, which is accompanied by singing.


Instrumental Music

An instrumental is a musical composition without lyrics or singing, although it might include some inarticulate vocal input. The music is primarily produced by musical instruments.


Western

It's lyrics depict trails and successes for everyday life. It is directly influenced by the folk music traditions of many cow boy songs .Reflect the realities in an open range through your voices.
